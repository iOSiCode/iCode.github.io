What is iCode?

iCode offers a full front-end online course on iOS development. A 50 hours course that is designed to make you know enough to start your journy in the iOS develpoment industry.
The course will be two weeks long starting December 27, 2020, from Sunday to Thursday, 10 AM to 2 PM on Discord app.

APP I
what would x say

This is a simple app with a single button and a single output field to get you started with iOS development basics. Once the buttons is tapped, a random phrase is generated.

APP II
Artist Quiz game

This is a game that will present a song name and the player gets to choose the image of the artist that created the song.

APP III
Flashing Cards

For this app you will create a flashing cards app. The user will input words and their definitions or descriptions, then a screen with many cards appears with words presented. Once a card is tapped you will make a flipping animation to go to the description side.

APP IV
Tic-tac-toe

It is a classic, we had to include it. However, the game you will create is undefeatable. During the creation process of this app, you will finally learn how to transition back and forth between screens.

APP V
Lyrics Finder

This is a simple lyrics finder app, where the user inputs an artist name and a song name then the app makes a call to an online database to get the lyrics and present it. This app will teach you how to link your apps to the internet to get the desired information.

APP VI
mytube

This will be our last app to create together, and it's the favorite of every developer. We will create an app that presents the videos of a playlist that you created on YouTube. And the good news is; we will also make the app fast and efficient.

APP VII
Peer Project

For this app you will be partnered with another student and you will be required to make an app using an API that will be given to you. You will make an app that can make calls to the internet and get some data.

APP VIII
Individual Project

Finally, you will put what you learned during this two weeks course to the test and show us your creativity. You will be asked to create any app of your own choosing with no rules or specifications.
